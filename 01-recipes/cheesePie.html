Cherry Cream Cheese Pie

This cherry cream cheese pie is one of my favorite and easiest recipes. It is always a hit at parties, bake sales, and potlucks. I have modified the original to decrease the fat and calories. It is still delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • â…“ cup lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1 (21 ounce) can light cherry pie filling

Steps

  1. Beat cream cheese until light and fluffy. Gradually stir in sweetened condensed milk and continue mixing until smooth and combined. Add lemon juice and vanilla; mix well.
  2. Pour filling into graham cracker crust. Refrigerate until set, 2 to 4 hours.
  3. Just before serving, spread cherry pie filling over the top.
